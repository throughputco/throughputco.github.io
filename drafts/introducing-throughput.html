Introducing Throughput

// under

Source

A few week ago, I listened to a lecture by David S. Black, Technology Partner at Oak Venture Partners about rapid iterations and software development techniques. In his mind, simply implementing Agile and Continuous Integration isn't enough to succeed in the highly competivite and volatile environment that we operate in.

This blog is an attempt to highlight the tools, techniques and resources being used by companies that are setting the standard for high performance cultures, so that we can learn from them.

Note: the term "high performance culture" can (and should) be attributed to Adrian Cockcroft, formerly Chief Architect at Netflix and now a Partner at Battery Ventures.
